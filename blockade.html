Iranian fast-attack craft and stationary platform-based missile batteries now shadow every commercial hull that dares to approach the 21-mile gap. The tactical reality on the water is one of constant near-misses; sonar operators on US destroyers report a "saturation of the acoustic environment" as thousands of small, autonomous underwater vehicles (UUVs) now litter the seafloor, acting as silent sentinels for both sides.
